About the Project

Analyzing bike stations

Hi! We're Abby, Joseph and Eric, three students at the University of Toronto in the Department of Geography and Planning and the creators of Active Transit Toronto. The goal of Active Transit Toronto (ATT) is to help Torontonians understand how accessible active transportation options, particularly biking, are in their area. Currently the majority of trips in Toronto are still made in cars, but this will need to change in order for Toronto to meet its climate change obligations. Many cities have created policy aimed at promoting modal shift towards public and active transit due to the greatly reduced greenhouse gas emissions (GHG) and increased efficiency that come with those modes. This tool aims to help Toronto residents understand the active transit options around them, what sort of car-free trips are possible and where developed bike infrastructure exists.
